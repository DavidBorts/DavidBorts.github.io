David Borts

I'm a researcher at Princeton's Computational Imaging Lab, advised by Professor Felix Heide. I also completed my undergraduate studies at Princeton, earning a degree in Computer Science with a minor in Neuroscience.

My research interests include neural graphics, scene reconstruction, and computational optics. I'm currently working on neural reconstruction methods for radar, as well as metasurface design for improved under display and hyperspectral cameras.
